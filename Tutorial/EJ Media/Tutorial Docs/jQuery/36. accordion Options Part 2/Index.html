Something to Ponder Over...

Our lives are limited.
The focus is on how much time it takes to make a decision...
In other words, the important thing is how quickly you can think.

Hence the Lesson...

Don't overthink things.
In my experience, people who are absorbed in their thoughts are just as easy to fool as people who don't think at all.
Think in moderation, act in moderation.
That's the lesson for you here.
